Build a RAG Based Gen-AI Application (chat-bot) - Q&A over YouTube Videos. Bot shall answer - Only from Context.

# 1 Select Topic of your interest  (e.g. Healthcare, Data Engineering, Gen-AI, or any Podcast videos).
